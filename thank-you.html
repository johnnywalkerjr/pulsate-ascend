How Pulsate Ascend Works

Get up an running with these quick and easy steps.

It takes under 30 minutes to integrate our plugin with an existing iOS or Android app.

Your App sends customer behaviors and location directly to Pulsate Ascend

You can now utilise Pulsate Ascend’s powerful tools to segment customers based on context and location and target customers with campaigns and offers they want to receive.

Segments

Group passengers into segments so that they can be targeted with highly relevant campaigns and offers.

Real-time Context

Send campaigns when customers want to receive them and are most likely to act on them. Send hotel deals when a user books a flight or tell them about your inflight offers when they are browsing airport stores.

Wearable SDK

Pulsate Ascend is looking to the future with full SDK support for both Apple and AndroidWear smartwatches. Send focused wearable campaigns to passengers to guide them through airport

Beacons and Geofences

Use Beacons and Geofences to send campaigns based on the customer’s current location. Send flight updates to customer’s when they arrive at the airport and car rental offers when they pass a beacon at their destination airport.

Powerful analytics

Measure the success of your campaigns and communications with Pulsate Ascend's powerful analytical tools. Set revenue and engagement goals for each campaign and track the uplift in sales and customer satisfaction.

CRM integration

Pulsate Ascend can be seamlessly integrated with existing CRM systems. All of your customer data from CRM can be utilised to build customer segments in Pulsate Ascend.
