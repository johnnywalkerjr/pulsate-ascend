Maximize Revenue from Add-Ons

Target passengers with specially selected services and add-ons designed to add value to their booking. Automate the sending offers to passengers based on their location and the actions they take in your app.

When a customer books a city break, automatically send them partner offers for great hotels in that city. When they disembark from the plane at their destination, send them offers for car rental and transport options to reach their destination.

As the passenger is exploring a new destination, target them with nearby partner offers for restaurants and attractions based on their current location.

Improve the Airport Experience

Use geofences to target customers who are on the way to the airport with flight and departure information and relevant info such as the average queue time to clear security. Remove a pain point by offering express security clearance, especially if the passenger is running late.

When the customer arrives at the boarding gate, a beacon can detect their presence and you can offer them priority boarding if they have not purchased it already.

Delight your passengers by automatically offering seat upgrades to loyal customers as soon as they enter the airport.

In Flight Upgrades

Target customers who are about to board the plane with a meal or entertainment upgrade. Pulsate Ascend can automatically check the user's segment data in real time and offer upgrades based on their previous purchase history.

For the user, it is as simple as confirming in the app and confirm in the requested meal will be delivered directly to their seat.
