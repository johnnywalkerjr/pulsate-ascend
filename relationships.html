Pulsate Ascend Deepens Passenger Relationships

Improve customer experience by removing passenger pain points and offering a better way to travel.

Real-Time Journey Updates

Deliver a personal push notification on the morning of your customer's flight with info about their destination including the expected weather conditions. Highlight great places to be experienced during their upcoming stay.

You can also remind passengers to leave themselves plenty of time to get to the airport and to bring their passport.

Guide Customers Through the Airport

Send passengers up to the minute estimates for clearing security and updates when the boarding gate opens. Direct passengers to the boarding gate with directions from their current location directly to the gate.

Stores and restaurants relevant to the user's previous purchase history can also be highlighted as they are navigating the airport. If a user has a smartwatch, these notifications can be sent directly to it to make the experience even more engaging and personal.

Send Real-Time Customer Alerts

Alert customers who are in danger of missing their flight with a push notification alert and direct them to their gate.

Send passengers queuing to board a campaign offering them the chance to purchase Priority Boarding and skip the queue.

Push notifications are a great way to inform loyal passengers that they have been upgraded.

Real-Time One to One Communication

Establish a direct dialogue with each customer. Every app user can user Pulsate Ascend to send a message directly to your customer service team if they have any problems or issues with their bookings. These queries can be replied to in real time to ensure the issue is addressed quickly.

Passengers can reply directly to any campaign to give you instant feedback. This real-time dialogue makes customers feel engaged with and ensures that issues are resolved quickly without the customer feeling the need to complain on social media or leave a negative review online.
